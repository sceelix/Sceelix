Surface Decompose

Decomposes a Surface into layers, all without destroying the links between the surface parts.

Inputs#

  • Input [Single | Surface]: Surface to be decomposed.

Outputs#

  • Output [Entity]: Surface subentities - the layers.
  • Original [Surface]: The original surface.
